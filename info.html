Purpose Of The Club

Our goal is simple - we aim to improve the practical software development skills of our members. To do this, we ask experienced developers who are in the field to come talk to our members; we provide workshops that teach skills that may not be taught in the classroom; and we provide an environment for our members to collaborate with their peers on projects that are essential to their development.
